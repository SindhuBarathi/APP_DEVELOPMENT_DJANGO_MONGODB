FAQ's

  • How do I contact customer care?

    In case you wish to contact customer care, please call us at 121 for queries/requests and 198 for complaints.Alternatively, you can write to us at voizfonica@gmail.com

  • I am unable to use Internet

    Login into your account and in contact us page raise a ticket.

  • I want to swap/upgrade my sim

    Please visit the nearest Voizfonica Store to swap/upgrade your SIM.

  • I want to update change my address

    We would be shortly coming up with the feature to change your address online. In the meantime, please visit the nearest VoizFonica Store to get your address changed.

  • Can I convert from Prepaid to Postpaid and vice versa?

    Yes, you can change your subscription from Prepaid to Post-paid or Post-paid to Prepaid. Please visit your nearest VoizFonica store with your valid Original Proof of Address(POA) / Proof of Identity(POI) or Aadhaar card.
